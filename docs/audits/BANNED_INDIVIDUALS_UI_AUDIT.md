## GOLD STANDARD PATTERNS (Reference)

### Card Styling
- **Card Container:** `backdrop-blur-xl bg-white/80 border-white/20 shadow-xl`
- **CardHeader:** Standard CardHeader component
- **CardTitle:** `text-xl font-semibold` or `text-lg font-semibold`
- **CardContent:** Standard CardContent component with `pt-6 px-6 pb-6`

### Button Styling
- **Primary Buttons:** `!bg-[#2563eb] hover:!bg-blue-700 text-white` or `bg-blue-600 hover:bg-blue-700 text-white`
- **Font:** `font-medium` (default) or `font-bold` for emphasis
- **Padding:** `px-4 py-2` (standard) or `px-6 py-3` (larger)
- **Icons:** `fas fa-*` with `mr-2` spacing

### Typography
- **Page Titles:** `text-3xl font-bold text-slate-900`
- **Section Titles:** `text-xl font-semibold` or `text-lg font-semibold`
- **Card Titles:** `text-xl font-semibold` or `text-lg font-semibold`
- **Body Text:** `text-sm text-slate-600` or `text-slate-600 font-medium`
- **Labels:** `text-sm font-medium text-slate-700` or `text-xs font-medium text-slate-700`

### Spacing
- **Section Spacing:** `space-y-6` (between major sections)
- **Card Padding:** `pt-6 px-6 pb-6` or `p-6`
- **Grid Gaps:** `gap-4` (standard), `gap-6` (larger)

### Icon Sizes
- **Card Icons:** `w-12 h-12` with `text-xl` or `text-2xl`
- **Small Icons:** `text-sm` or `text-lg`
- **Button Icons:** `text-lg` or `text-xl` with `mr-2`
